Course Experience

Before The Course


Before taking an online course on keyboard and music, a person may have little to no experience with playing musical instruments. They may be curious about music and its technicalities, but lack the confidence and knowledge to fully dive in. They may struggle with understanding musical terms, notes, and rhythm, and feel intimidated by the complexity of playing the keyboard. Without any prior training or exposure, they may feel overwhelmed and unsure of where to begin. However, with a desire to learn and grow, they are ready to take the plunge and explore the world of music through an online course.

During The Course


Pursuing an online course on keyboard and music can evoke a range of emotions in a person. At first, they may feel confused and overwhelmed by the amount of information presented to them. They may struggle to understand the musical terminology, how to read sheet music, and how to coordinate their hands on the keyboard. This confusion may cause them to doubt their ability to learn and may lead to frustration. However, with time, practice, and patience, they will begin to feel more confident in their abilities. As they progress through the course, they may feel a sense of accomplishment as they start to recognize the songs they play and see improvement in their skills.

To help motivate someone who is feeling confused, it's important to remind them that it's normal to feel this way when starting something new. It's also important to encourage them to take things one step at a time and to celebrate small victories along the way. Practicing regularly, even if it's just for a few minutes a day, can help them progress faster and build their confidence. It's also helpful to remind them of the benefits of learning to play an instrument, such as reducing stress, improving cognitive function, and providing a creative outlet. Finally, reminding them that learning an instrument is a journey, not a destination, can help them stay motivated and focused on their progress rather than their perceived shortcomings.

After The Course


After completing an online course on keyboard and music, a person would feel a sense of accomplishment and pride in their newly acquired skills. They would have a deeper understanding of musical concepts, be able to read sheet music, and play a variety of songs on the keyboard. They may also feel more confident in their ability to learn and tackle new challenges. This newfound skill can provide a sense of fulfillment and joy, and may even lead to a lifelong passion for music. Overall, completing an online course on keyboard and music can be a transformative experience that leaves a person feeling more skilled, accomplished, and inspired.
