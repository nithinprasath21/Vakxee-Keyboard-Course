About Us

Hi, I'm Vishal Nagarajan, but you might know me better by my stage name, Vakxee. I'm a keyboardist with over seven years of experience, and I'm passionate about sharing my love of music with others.

In my early years, I found my love for music, and I began to learn the keyboard. Since then, I have been constantly working on improving my skills and exploring different musical styles. Currently, I am a member of my college band, which has given me many opportunities to perform in front of live audiences.

As a musician, I believe that music has the power to connect people, evoke emotions, and create unforgettable memories. And that is why I am excited to share my knowledge and experience through an online course on keyboard.

My goal is to make learning the keyboard fun, engaging, and accessible to anyone who is willing to learn. I will guide students through the fundamentals of playing the keyboard, including techniques, music theory, and improvisation. And through practice and dedication, I believe that my students can achieve the same level of proficiency that I have reached.

I am confident that my course will inspire and motivate my students to explore their creativity, develop their skills, and ultimately fall in love with music just as I have. So, let's start this journey together and make some beautiful music!
